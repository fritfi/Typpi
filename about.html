About Typpi

Empowering learners through active typing practice

Our Mission

Typpi is a modern typing practice platform designed to help students, professionals, and lifelong learners improve their typing skills while enhancing information retention. We believe that actively retyping notes and study materials is one of the most effective ways to learn and remember information.

What is Typpi?

Typpi transforms the passive act of reading notes into an active learning experience. By requiring users to type out their study materials, we engage multiple cognitive processes that strengthen memory formation and improve recall. Our platform combines proven learning techniques with modern web technology to create an effective, enjoyable practice environment.

Key Features

Practice Mode

Our core feature allows you to paste any text—lecture notes, textbook excerpts, vocabulary lists, or code snippets—and practice typing it out. The interface provides real-time feedback, tracks your progress, and displays your typing speed (WPM) and accuracy.

Race Mode

Challenge yourself with our competitive race mode, where you can test your typing speed against the clock. Perfect for building muscle memory and improving your typing efficiency.

Cloud Synchronization

Sign in with your Google account to sync your practice sessions and progress across all your devices. Your notes and statistics are securely stored and accessible whenever you need them.

Customizable Options

Tailor your practice experience with options for case sensitivity and punctuation. Whether you're focusing on speed or accuracy, Typpi adapts to your learning goals.

The Science Behind Active Typing

Research in cognitive psychology has consistently shown that active recall and repetition are among the most effective learning strategies. When you retype your notes:

  • Encoding: The act of typing engages motor memory and reinforces neural pathways
  • Active Engagement: You're forced to process each word, rather than passively skimming
  • Repetition: Multiple exposures to the material strengthen long-term retention
  • Focus: The typing interface minimizes distractions and keeps you engaged

Our Story

Typpi was created by Prithvee Arora, a developer and student who recognized the power of active note-taking. Frustrated with traditional flashcard apps and passive reading, Prithvee built Typpi to combine typing practice with effective learning strategies. What started as a personal tool has grown into a platform used by learners around the world.
